23 May 2026V1.6.7 — 1.6.8
Food crate (crate_normal_2_food) now rolls 2–3 times with updated quantities (1–4 per item), water items capped at 1 and cannot stack with each other.

21 May 2026V1.5.4 — 1.6.6
Enabled crate_basic, crate_basic_jungle, crate_mine, crate_normal and crate_tools with the new double-clear loot system. Each crate now has a distinct loot structure with weighted rolls and category guarantees.
Changed how the plugin hooks into the game's loot system to prevent interfering with crate initialization. This fixes a potential issue where the plugin could block other crate-related systems from running correctly.
Patrol helicopter crates have been completely restructured. Each crate now guarantees a weapon and two ammo types, with a 40% chance of finding a rocket, C4, MLRS rocket or explosives, and a 10% chance of an advanced blueprint fragment.
Basic box crates (crate_normal_2) have been enabled and restructured. Each crate guarantees a component alongside a weighted roll — 80% chance of weapons, grenades, armor or tools, and 20% chance of electronics or deployables.
Vehicle parts containers are now active with the custom loot system, dropping two unique engine parts alongside guaranteed scrap.
New chinook hackable crates logic have been temporarily disabled while the map marker issue is being investigated.

CalmProtection new
v1.2.9
3 Jun 2026V1.2.9
/ao command now shows approximate offline time (e.g. "around 6-7 hours") instead of exact time.
Players offline less than 10 minutes now show as "Just logged out".
Players in cooldown period (under 10 min offline) now correctly show "Not protected".
24 May 2026V1.1.1 — 1.2.8
+Added TC-based group protection — protection now applies when all players authorized on the nearest Tool Cupboard are offline, not just the entity owner.
+Added fallback to owner-based protection when no TC is found within range.
+Added earliest TC priority — when multiple TCs cover the same area, the one placed first is used.
+Added solo/team label to /ao command output.
+Added teammate online detection to /ao — shows "Not protected, teammate online" instead of protection percentage when a teammate is still connected.
Fixed errors related to TC authorized players list access and type mismatches.
