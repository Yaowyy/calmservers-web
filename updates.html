20 May 2026
−Removed longsword, ballista.mounted, ballista.static, batteringram, catapult, rf.detonator, smart.alarm, smart.switch, storage.monitor, electric.counter, electric.hbhfsensor, electric.laserdetector, electric.digitalclock, electric.andswitch, electrical.memorycell, electric.random.switch, electric.rf.broadcaster, electric.rf.receiver, electric.seismicsensor from military crate.

+Added workbench upgrades (accelerated, comfort, defensive, efficiency, prototype, range, recyclebin, reinforced, salvage, surplus) to military crate.
+Added guaranteed crude oil (15–25) and low grade fuel (8–15) to oil barrels — both items always drop.
+Added guaranteed blowpipe to jungle crates alongside 1 dart ammo and 1 medical/other item.
+Added guaranteed rocket or C4 and guaranteed ammo to patrol helicopter crates.
+Added 10% chance of advanced blueprint fragment to patrol helicopter crates.
+Added chainsaw, jackhammer as guaranteed tool to supply drop.
+Added guaranteed explosive (satchel or C4) and guaranteed explosive to supply drop.

Military crate restructured with guaranteed armor, component and electronic per drop, plus 50% chance of weapon.
Supply drop restructured with guaranteed picks from weapons, clothing, components, ammo, explosives, tools and weapon mods.
Heli crate restructured with guaranteed rocket/C4, guaranteed ammo, plus 2 random rolls.
Crate mine now rolls twice with 40% chance tools, 60% chance resources per roll.
