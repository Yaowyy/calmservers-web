Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This website (calmservers.com and calmservers.online) does not use cookies, does not track visitors, and does not collect any personal data. You can browse this site completely anonymously.

Who We Are

EU Calm is a community Rust game server operated by an individual based in the United Kingdom. This privacy policy applies to the static landing pages hosted at calmservers.com and calmservers.online.

Data We Collect

We collect no personal data whatsoever through this website. There are no contact forms, no login systems, no newsletter signups, and no analytics tracking. Visiting this site does not result in any personal information being stored by us.

Cookies

This website does not use cookies of any kind — neither functional, analytical, nor marketing cookies. No cookie consent is required because no cookies are set.

Third-Party Services

This site links to the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy:

Discord — our community is hosted on Discord. If you join our Discord server, Discord's own privacy policy applies. You can read it at discord.com/privacy.

Steam / Valve — the "Play on Steam" button uses a steam:// protocol link which opens the Steam client on your device. No data is sent to us when you click it. Steam's privacy policy applies to your Steam account and can be found at store.steampowered.com/privacy_agreement.

Google Fonts — this site loads fonts from Google Fonts. Google may log the request to serve the font files. See Google's privacy policy for details.

GitHub Pages

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc.). GitHub may collect basic server logs including IP addresses as part of operating their hosting infrastructure. This is outside our control. See GitHub's privacy statement for details.

Your Rights Under GDPR

As we hold no personal data about you, there is nothing for us to access, correct, delete, or export on your behalf. If you have any privacy-related questions, you can contact us via our Discord server at discord.gg/3hkgmwPm28.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever add features that involve data collection (such as analytics or contact forms), this policy will be updated accordingly before those features go live.
